About the festival

Where nature, wellness and culture meet.

Bloom Fest is a two-day cultural festival that brings together art, creative industries, education, public engagement, installation performance and a curated luxury collection. Together they present Georgia's natural mineral wealth and position the country as an international hub of natural regeneration.

Almost every resort in Georgia takes part, alongside local entrepreneurs and representatives of the creative industries. The 2026 edition expands across balneological and wellness therapies, art, culture, tourism, modern approaches to healthy living, medicine and education.

International speakers

Voices from the global wellness field.

Sergio Gianesini
Italy · Medical science

Founder of The Science Per Aquam project. Professor at the University of Ferrara and associate professor at USUHS, USA. Vice-president of the International Union of Phlebology and executive board member of the American College of Phlebology. President of the Venous and Lymphatic World International Network Foundation.

At Bloom Fest he presents a contemporary rehabilitation course using thermal waters for the treatment of chronic venous disease.

Miyu Yoda
Japan · Wellness media

Japanese wellness blogger with half a million international subscribers. She will cover the festival for a global audience.

Her focus: Georgia as a wellness destination, natural methods, thermal waters, spa and relaxation, and the role of nature-based therapies in modern society.

2025 · Successful pilot

A proven format, ready to scale.

Bloom Festival was held for the first time at the Tbilisi Balneological Spa Resort in 2025. Over four thousand guests attended across two days of B2B meetings, professional lectures, workshops and a natural-product fair. Several Georgian companies signed supply contracts with hotels and resorts that now stock their products in spa facilities.
